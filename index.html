Welcome. I am Katherin Williams

After being a stay-at-home mom for 15 years, I decided to go back to school after my husband came out of the U.S. Navy 100% disabled. I graduated with a BS in Web Development, December 2019 with honors from Franklin University. However, covid circumstances created a need for me to help my last child graduate from high school through lockdowns and course changes. I am now looking for a fulltime position where I can learn more, make websites and apps look amazing and function beautifully, all while keeping shareholders happy.

I love the designing phase and love to make websites and pages that speak to the viewer. I find it very rewarding to bring the audience what they are looking for, especially when they were not sure what they want until they see the creation I made for them. I have learned how to write code using HTML, CSS, SQL, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. I also have experience creating on Wordpress, databases using MySQL and SQLite, and MVC websites using Ruby and Ruby on Rails and PHP and Symfony. Photoshop is truly a love of mine, as I have created posters, t-shirts, logo's, invitations and much more. I also have added Adobe XD and Adobe Illustrator to create UI/UX projects.

I currently am learning React and all the in's and out's of Product Owners in the hopes of achieving the Scrum Alliance Product Owner Certification. My other interests: I am an excellent cook and baker, love photography, sewing, home remodeling, and equestrian care.
